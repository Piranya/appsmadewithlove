Apps for iOS made by Mykola & Milana Tkachenko

Robotok

Assemble robots from parts. Simple app for kids woth the goal to assemble different robots from parts. No distraction, no ads - just infinite looped gameplay.

Animalubra

3 in 1 puzzle game for kids. Match animals images with its shadows in 3 different gameplays: - select correct shadow for presented animal; - move all animals to its shadows; - draw a line connecting animal with its shadow.

Animalubra Lite

Free version of Animalubra app that is all the same but restricting number of available animals to 20(including unicorn :)).
