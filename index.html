Robotok

Assemble robots from parts. Simple app for kids with the goal to assemble different robots from parts. No distraction, no ads - just infinite looped gameplay.

Animalumbra

3 in 1 puzzle game for kids. Match animals images with its shadows in 3 different gameplays:
- select correct shadow for presented animal;
- move all animals to its shadows;
- draw a line connecting animal with its shadow.

AnimalUmbra Pair

Find a pair game for kids. Infinite memo game.

AnimalUmbra Line

Draw a line connecting every animal, bird or bug to its shadow.

Animalumbra Grid

Drag all animals, birds and bugs to their shadows. There will be more objects with each level.

Shadow Ring

Match shadow game for children. Find the right shadow in the circle and drop your animal there. Get to know different animals, birds and bugs.

Colour Pairs Memo

Find a pair game for kids. Infinite memo game with 8 basic colours.
